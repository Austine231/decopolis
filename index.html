Welcome to Decopolis Enterprises

Climate‑smart aquaculture for a food‑secure Kenya

We are a registered green aquaculture company operating modern floating fish cages on Lake Victoria, Busia County. Our mission is to produce high‑quality Tilapia using environmentally responsible methods, while creating jobs and supporting the Blue Economy.

📊 120+ tons annual production | 🐟 20+ cages in Year 1 | 👥 40+ local jobs

About Us

Driving sustainable change in the Lake Victoria basin

Our Mission

To produce affordable, high-quality fish through environmentally responsible aquaculture practices that promote food security, economic growth, and ecological sustainability.
