About Decopolis

Decopolis Enterprises Limited is a sustainable aquaculture enterprise operating along Lake Victoria in Busia County, Kenya. We combine modern fish cage farming with responsible environmental stewardship, directly contributing to food security, blue economy growth, and community resilience.

Our core business integrates climate-smart floating fish cages, water quality monitoring, low-stocking density ethics, and zero-waste feed management. We produce premium Tilapia and other commercial species while actively reducing pressure on wild fish stocks.

“Sustainable Fish. Thriving Communities. Healthy Waters.”

Vision: To become one of East Africa’s leading sustainable aquaculture companies within 10 years, driving blue economy growth while protecting aquatic ecosystems.

Mission: Produce affordable, high-quality fish through environmentally responsible practices that promote food security, economic growth, and ecological sustainability.

Triple Bottom Line

🌱 Planet: Sustainable cage farming · water quality monitoring · reduced overfishing · responsible feed conversion.

👥 People: Youth & women employment · local fish trader income · training & community uplift.

💰 Profit: Scalable production · value-added fish products · strong regional demand.
